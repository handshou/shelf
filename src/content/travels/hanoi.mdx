## Charging

Vietnam can have two types of power sockets, and they can supply either 110V or 220V so be sure to check your appliance thoroughly. We did not find 110V appliances during our visit.

I believe the use of these plugs are from French, American and Japanese influence on Vietnam.

## Phone data plans, prices

**Prioritise this before the trip to the airport.** I recommend getting an eSIM ahead of your trip, you can make purchase any time, but the activation pre-requisites include having a reliable connection + familiarity with your phone's settings.

I found reddit recommendations to have the best island coverage with **Vinaphone**. For a 4-day trip, I bought the 7 GB data plan and it was well in excess!

Wifi at the accomodation wasn't the best, and I was uploading multiple 10 MB jpgs daily. Purchasing an eSIM plan from a reliable network was well worth it.
